# v0.3 Homepage Specification

## Version

v0.3 — Landing Page Foundation

## Purpose

This document defines the structure and content requirements for the first professional homepage of Belarus Digital Human Rights Lab.

The homepage should present the Lab as a long-term digital platform while clearly identifying the first active module: Forced Labour in Places of Detention.

## Homepage Structure

### 1. Header

The header should include:

- Project name: Belarus Digital Human Rights Lab
- Navigation:
  - Home
  - Map
  - Research
  - Data
  - About
- Language switcher:
  - BE
  - EN
  - RU

### 2. Hero Section

The hero section should include:

- Main title: Belarus Digital Human Rights Lab
- Tagline: Verified Evidence. Digital Research. Human Rights.
- Short mission statement
- Primary call-to-action: Explore the Map

### 3. Current Module

The homepage should clearly show the first active module:

Forced Labour in Places of Detention

This module focuses on:

- prison labour practices;
- wages and remuneration;
- occupational safety and health;
- food and nutrition;
- pension and social insurance rights;
- production linked to military supply chains;
- legal framework and international standards;
- verified testimonies of former prisoners.

### 4. Map Preview

The homepage should include a preview block for the interactive map of Belarusian places of detention.

At this stage, the block may use a placeholder until the full interactive map is implemented.

### 5. Key Research Areas

The homepage should present key research areas:

- Wages
- Occupational Safety
- Food and Nutrition
- Pension Rights
- Military Production
- Legal Analysis

### 6. Evidence and Data

The homepage should explain that the platform will include:

- verified testimonies;
- anonymised datasets;
- research methodology;
- source protection principles;
- open data where appropriate.

### 7. Reports

The homepage should include a section for future research reports and analytical publications.

### 8. Footer

The footer should include:

- © 2026 Belarus Digital Human Rights Lab
- language options;
- future links to GitHub, contact information and citation policy.

## Design Requirements

The homepage should be:

- clear;
- minimalistic;
- serious;
- readable;
- responsive;
- suitable for international human rights, research and advocacy audiences.

## Notes

This specification is the basis for implementing website version v0.3.
